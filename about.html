Background

I've always been very technologically inclined showing a huge interest in toys and machines. I always found myself wondering what it took to make a real world machine to move and do something cool. Being interested in a field like robotics, I found I could make use of my knowledge in math and sciences in order to solve big world problems. In highschool I founded the Project FX Autonomous Robotics team which ended up being one of the most successful autonomous robotics teams in Southern Ontario. With optimism in mind, I see myself moving towards building the next big thing in the field of intelligent robots and tackling today's biggest issues!
